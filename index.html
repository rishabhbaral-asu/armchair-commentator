Injury Report: The Rana Setback

The biggest news out of the Indian camp is the confirmed withdrawal of Harshit Rana. A side-strain sustained during the final warmup match against UAE has forced the selectors' hand. While many hoped for Rana’s raw pace to unsettle the American top order, India has turned back to the "Miyan Magic"—Mohammed Siraj. Siraj’s experience in high-pressure ICC knockouts provides a tactical floor that Rana’s high-ceiling mystery simply couldn't match right now.

Tactical Prediction: The "Netravalkar" Effect

Expect USA to bowl first if they win the toss. They know the evening dew at Wankhede turns the surface into a skid-fest. Saurabh Netravalkar will likely target Abhishek Sharma’s outside edge early. If USA can pinch two wickets in the first four overs, the middle-order battle between Hardik Pandya and the USA’s spin-duo (Harmeet & Van Schalkwyk) becomes a grind India isn't used to.
