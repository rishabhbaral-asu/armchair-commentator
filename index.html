Injury Report: The Rana Setback

In a crushing blow to India’s pace-bowling rotation, Harshit Rana has been officially ruled out after sustaining a side strain in the warmup match against the UAE. The BCCI has called up Mohammed Siraj to lead the attack alongside Bumrah. While Siraj offers Wankhede familiarity, India loses the raw, hit-the-deck pace that Rana was expected to use to hurry the USA top order.

Can History Repeat?

The USA isn't just here to participate. Having famously defeated Pakistan in the 2024 World Cup, this unit knows how to handle subcontinent pressure. Saurabh Netravalkar, returning to his original home ground, will be the primary threat to Abhishek Sharma and Ishan Kishan early on.
