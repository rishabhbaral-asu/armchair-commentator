The Pace Pivot: Siraj over Rana

In a late-breaking squad adjustment, Mohammed Siraj has replaced Harshit Rana in the final 15-man Indian roster. This move signals Gautam Gambhir's desire for "Proven Heat" over "Fresh Mystery." Siraj’s ability to extract wobble at the Wankhede under lights offers India a safety net that Rana’s raw pace couldn't yet guarantee on the world stage.

The Death-Over Chess Match

Tactically, the USA will target the 12th-16th over window. With Harmeet Singh and Van Schalkwyk, they aim to "bore" the Indian middle order into mistakes. The battle between Suryakumar Yadav’s 360-degree range and Ali Khan’s reverse-swinging yorkers will likely decide the winner. If USA can drag the game into the final over, Netravalkar’s left-arm angle against Rinku Singh becomes the most expensive chess move in T20 history.
