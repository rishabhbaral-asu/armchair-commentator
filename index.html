The Narrative: Levi's Stadium plays host to a matchup of unexpected destinies. The Seattle Seahawks, led by the career renaissance of Sam Darnold (4,200 yards, 32 TDs), face the New England Patriots and their sophomore sensation Drake Maye.

The Matchup: Seattle's offense is a vertical machine. DK Metcalf and Jaxon Smith-Njigba have tormented secondaries all season. They face their stiffest test in Patriots' corner Christian Gonzalez, an All-Pro who erased WR1s throughout the AFC playoffs.

The Strategy: New England must control the clock. Running back Rhamondre Stevenson is the key to keeping Darnold off the field. Seattle's head coach Mike Macdonald will likely stack the box, daring the young Drake Maye to beat them over the top.

The Prediction: Experience matters on the grandest stage. While Maye has been brilliant, Darnold has the weapons and the defense to edge out a victory. Expect a close game, but Seattle pulls away late.
